About Us

Building a Healthier Pet Community.

Pawpatrol was founded by a passionate team of lifelong animal lovers and visionary veterinarians. Their shared dream was to create a medical haven of knowledge and care accessible to all pets. United by their belief in the transformational power of compassionate care, they embarked on a journey to build this clinic. With relentless dedication, they gathered a team of experts and launched this modern facility, creating a community of pet parents all connected by the desire to see their furry friends explore, live, and grow happily.

Meet Our Lead Veterinarian

Dr. Jhonny Sins

Lead Veterinarian & Founder

With over 15 years of dedicated experience in small animal medicine, Dr. Sins founded PawPatrol with a single mission: to provide world-class, fear-free veterinary care in a welcoming, comfortable environment.

He received his Doctorate of Veterinary Medicine with honors and specializes in preventative care, soft-tissue surgery, and specialized feline medicine. When he isn't at the clinic treating his favorite patients, you can find him hiking local trails with his two rescue golden retrievers.

"My goal isn't just to treat your pet when they are sick. It is to partner with you to ensure they live the longest, happiest life possible."
